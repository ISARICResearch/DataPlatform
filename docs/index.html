Welcome to the ISARIC Data Platform

The official repository for the ISARIC Data Platform, a collaborative and patient-oriented hub designed to drive global clinical studies in the face of severe infections and rapidly emerging public health threats.

Our Platform Serves Three Pivotal Roles:

  • Data Capture: Ensuring the integrity and reliability of data from the outset, this segment is dedicated to the meticulous acquisition of clinical data.
  • Data Processing: Focused on data transformation and preparation, this area is key to maintaining data quality and making it suitable for in-depth analysis.
  • Data Analysis: Here, we delve into the processed data to unearth vital insights, employing state-of-the-art analytical techniques to inform decision-making and contribute to life-saving knowledge.

The ISARIC Data Platform is at the forefront of tackling critical health challenges. By fostering a collaborative environment, we support the development and execution of crucial research protocols.

Our Goal:

Our goal is to maximize the availability of clinical information and generate new knowledge, thereby having a profound impact on public health and saving lives. This repository is the heart of our collaborative efforts, documenting and supporting the development of tools and methodologies that empower our mission.
